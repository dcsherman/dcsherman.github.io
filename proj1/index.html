Nava-rasa: the "Nine Sentiments" of Indian aesthetics

A rasa is the artistic representation of an emotional state, and the goal of classical Indian literature, painting, oratory or performance art. Rasas are created through a wide range of means, and the ancient Indian texts discuss many such means. For example, one way is through the use of gestures and facial expressions of the actors. The theory of rasas forms the aesthetic underpinning of all Indian classical dance and theatre, such as Bharatanatyam, Kathakali, Kathak, Kuchipudi, Odissi, Manipuri, Kudiyattam, and others.
